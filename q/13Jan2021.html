Q: 求积分 ∫01arctan⁡(x)arcsin⁡(x)x2dx.

A: 试了试推广: 求积分∫01arctan⁡(kx)arcsin⁡xx2dx. 记积分为I(k), 则

I′(k)=∫01arcsin⁡xx(1+k2x2)dx=∫01arcsin⁡xxdx−∫01k2xarcsin⁡x(1+k2x2)dx=πlog⁡22−∫01k2xarcsin⁡x(1+k2x2)dx=πlog⁡22−12arcsin⁡xlog⁡(1+k2x2)|01+12∫01log⁡(1+k2x2)1−x2dx=πlog⁡22−πlog⁡(1+k2)4+12∫01log⁡(1+k2x2)1−x2dx.

记积分J(k)=12∫01log⁡(1+k2x2)1−x2dx, 求导得

J′(k)=∫01x2k(1+k2x2)1−x2dx=∫0π/2ksin2⁡t1+k2sin2⁡tdt=π2k−1k∫0π/2dt1+k2sin2⁡t=π2k−1k1+k2∫0π/2d1+k2tan⁡t1+(1+k2)tan2⁡t=π2(1k−1k1+k2).

因此

∫J′(k)=π2(log⁡k+coth−1⁡1+k2)+C=π2log⁡(k2+1+1)+C.

再由 J(0)=0 得 J(k)=π2(log⁡(k2+1+1)−log⁡2). 因此求得 I′(k)=π2log⁡k2+1+1k2+1. 故,

I(k)=π2(klog⁡k2+1+1k2+1−arctan⁡(k)+sinh−1⁡(k)).

计算得

∫01arctan⁡xarcsin⁡xx2dx=−π8(π−4log⁡(2+32)).
