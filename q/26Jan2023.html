Q: R2 上的通常加法为 (a,b)+(c,d)=(a+c,b+d), 我们知道 (R2,+) 是一个群. 问题: 有几种 × 的定义, 使得 (R2,+,×) 是一个环?

  • 直积 (a,b)×(c,d)=(ac,bd)

  • C 的 (a,b)×(c,d)=(ac−bd,ad+bc)

  • Dual number (a,b)×(c,d)=(ac,ad+bc)

这是 3 种 × 的定义.

A: 这就是二维 R-代数分类, 把 R2 嵌入 R2×2 中即可. 最后的结果由实矩阵的最简形式分类.

Q: 对应于对角矩阵 (ab), 对称矩阵(abba), 反对称矩阵 (a−bba) 均为 R2×2 的二维子环?

A: Let {a,b} be a basis of A, a 2-dimensional R-algebra. Here we can also regard a and b as 2×2 real matrices acting on some fixed basis. Denote χM as characteristic polynomial of matrix M.

If dim⁡χa=1, then we set a=1 without the loss of generality. Since {a,b} are linearly independent, dim⁡χb=2. Such A is isomorphic to R[X]/(X2+cX+d), which is isomorphic to

  • R[X]/(X2+1)≃C for Δ<0,

  • R[X]/(X2)≃dual number for Δ=0,

  • R[X]/(X2−1)≃split C for Δ>0.

Consider the case dim⁡χa=dim⁡χb=2. Whenever both are non-diagonalisable, or both are diagonalisable, then there exists μ,λ∈R such that λa+μb=1. This is what we have discussed previously.

Now we focus on dim⁡χa=dim⁡χb=2, and a is diagonalisable yet b is not. Since a∈spanR(b,b2), a commutes with b. If we write a=λb+μb2, then λ=0. Thus A≃C.
